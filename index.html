About Me

I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Immersive Media & Game Development at Temasek Polytechnic. I enjoy working on projects that combine my creativity with real-world purpose, whether it’s building educational games, designing intuitive user interfaces, or creating immersive 3D environments.

I am motivated by work that has impact, especially in areas related to sustainability, community support, and mental health. I am a resilient and resourceful individual who thrives in dynamic environments and embraces challenges as opportunities for growth. I am eager to learn and contribute meaningfully to every task undertaken.
